Ambulance Dashboard will have 2 main sections:
1. Ambulance Registration: This section will allow ambulance owners to register their ambulances by providing necessary details such as registration number, owner name, ownership type, contact number, service area, and active status. Additionally, they can add specific details about the ambulance such as vehicle name, picture, NICU/ICU availability, and service area.
2. Ambulance Management: This section will enable ambulance owners to manage their registered ambulances. They can view the list of their ambulances, update details, and change the active status as needed. This section will also provide options for filtering and searching ambulances based on various criteria such as service area and NICU/ICU availability.
3. Update Ambulance Details: This section will allow ambulance owners to update the details of their registered ambulances. They can modify information such as vehicle name, picture, NICU/ICU availability, service area, and active status. This feature ensures that the ambulance information remains accurate and up-to-date for users seeking ambulance services.
4. Update Ambulance Admin Details: This section will allow ambulance owners to update their personal details such as owner name, contact number, and ownership type. This ensures that the contact information for the ambulance is current and accurate for users who may need to reach out for services.
